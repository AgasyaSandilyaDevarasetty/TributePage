Swami Vivekananda

An Indian Hindu Monk

Time line of Swami Vivekananda life:

  • Born:12 January, 1863
  • Place of Birth:Kolkata,India
  • Birth Name:Narendranath Dutta
  • Education:Calcutta Metropolitan School; Presidency College, Calcutta
  • Religion:Hinduism
  • Founder of: Ramakrishna Mission (1897), Ramakrishna Math, Vedanta Society of New York
  • Literary works:Raja Yoga (1896), Karma Yoga (1896), Bhakti Yoga (1896), Jnana Yoga, My Master (1901), Lectures from Colombo to Almora (1897)
  • Death:4 July, 1902
  • Place of Death:Belur Math, Belur, Bengal
  • Memorial:Belur Math. Belur, West Bengal

Swami Vivekananda was an inspiring personality and was famous in whole world. He was born on 12 January, 1863, Kolkata (earlier Calcutta). He was a spiritual leader and social reformer. His lectures, writings, letters, poems, ideas motivated not only youth of India but also whole world. He is the founder of Ramakrishna Mission and Belur Math in Calcutta, which are still working towards helping the needy. He was the man of wisdom and a very simple human being.

Arise, awake and stop not until the goal is achieved --Swami Vivekananda
